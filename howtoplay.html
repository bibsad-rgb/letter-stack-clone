☕ HOW TO PLAY ☕

☕ LETTER STACK ☕

Objective

Discover the hidden word by guessing the missing letters

on both sides of the given letters.

Rules

  1. Start with Clues: You'll see a partial word with some letters revealed in the middle.

  2. Guess Letters: Try to figure out which letters come before and after the given letters to form a complete word.

  3. Fill the Blanks: Each blank space represents a missing letter. Guess one letter at a time.

Example

If you see: _ _ A T _ _

The answer could be: B E A T S

You need to guess B, E, and S to complete the word!
